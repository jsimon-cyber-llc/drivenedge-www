01 About Who we are

Built around the work, with the edge to see it through.

Driven Edge is a technology and AI consultancy. We advise, design, build, and run modern software — and the AI woven through it — across the full life of a system: deciding what should exist, engineering it to hold up under real use, and keeping it sharp as the world changes. The name is the posture. Driven is the standard we hold the work to; edge is the difference you feel in something made with care.

We move in senior teams, close to the problem and accountable for the outcome. There is no handoff to a thinner version of us once the contract is signed — the people who design and build a thing are the people who answer for it. That continuity is the whole point: software worth keeping is software someone is still standing behind.
