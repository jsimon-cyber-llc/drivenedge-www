Privacy Policy

Last updated 25 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Driven Edge LLC ("Driven Edge", "we", "us") handles information in connection with this website, drivenedge.net (the "Site"). We keep things deliberately simple: this Site is a static informational page, and we collect as little as possible.

Information we collect

The Site does not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or third-party analytics, and it has no sign-up or contact forms. We do not collect personal information from you simply by visiting.

  • Email you send us. If you email hello@drivenedge.net, we receive the address and any details you choose to include, and we use them solely to read and respond to your message.
  • Standard server logs. Our hosting and content-delivery providers may automatically record routine technical data (such as IP address, browser type, and the pages requested) to operate, secure, and troubleshoot the Site. This is standard for virtually all websites.
  • Web fonts. The Site loads typefaces from Google Fonts, which may process your IP address to deliver the font files. See Google's privacy policy for details.

How we use information

We use the limited information above only to operate and protect the Site and to respond to messages you send us. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising.

Sharing

We share information only with service providers who help us run the Site (for example, our hosting and content-delivery providers), and only as needed for them to provide those services, or where required by law.

Data retention

We retain email correspondence for as long as needed to address your inquiry and to keep reasonable business records, after which it is deleted.

Your choices

You may contact us at any time to ask what correspondence we hold about you, or to request that we delete it. Because the Site sets no cookies and runs no analytics, there is nothing to opt out of while browsing.

Children

The Site is intended for a general business audience and is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@drivenedge.net. Driven Edge LLC is a company organized in the United States.
